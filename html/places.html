Places To See

Quaker Street Cafe

Whether you need to start your day with coffee (like me!), or you want to grab lunch while ticking off all the free things to do in Shoreditch, then you should do it at Quaker Street Cafe!

Petticoat Lane

Petticoat Lane market has over a thousand stalls, specialising in clothes and household goods. The bulk of the market falls within the Tower Hamlets area with Middlesex Street only in the City.

Ballie Ballerson

Welcome to the EPIC original ball pit cocktail bar! The incredible Ballie Ballerson is like no other bar you’ve ever been to. A world famous venue featuring over ONE MILLION balls, it will magic you right back to the fun of the playground like a ball pit time machine. Whether you’re being a baller in our VIP section, drinking Dibbie Dabbersons on the dance floor, or making a name for yourself in one of the ball pits, it’s an experience that will keep you smiling for hours and have your Insta feed begging for more.

Kingly Court

Kingly Court £-££ Carnaby’s iconic is a three-storey alfresco food and dining destination in the heart of London’s West End. The unique food hub brings 21 of the best international concept restaurants, bars and cafés situated in a vibrant courtyard, which is open air throughout the summer months and covered in the winter.

Beigel Bake

Open 24 hours a day every day, including Christmas Day(!), Beigel Bake is an East-London institution. And it has been since it opened its doors in 1974. Their bagels are ridiculously cheap, ridiculously tasty, and the service is ridiculously friendly!

Dennis Server

Billed as a “still-life drama”, the five-story house was created by the previous owner Dennis Severs and offers a historical imagination of what life would have been like for a family of Huguenot silk weavers.

Cereal Killer Cafe

Among the decor are novelty cereal boxes, vintage milk bottles and other cereal related memorabilia. Topping off the old-school vibe is music from the 80s and 90s.

Cupcakes and shht

Very little cute cafe, they make delicious home made dishes, vegan options are very tasty too, a great find in the outskirts of shoreditch
